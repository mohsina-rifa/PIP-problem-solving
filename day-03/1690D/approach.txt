difficulty level : 1000







OPERATION:

re-color  : W ==> B





TASK:

consecutive black cells   : length = k




APPROACH:
Sliding-Window

=> count 'W'  : withinin the first k characters
    minimum-whites  : initialize with count.

=> move k-length window   :   one position at a time
    decrement whites  :   if the outgoing char is 'W'
    increment         :   if the incoming char is 'W'
      track the minimum whites seen.

=> print minimum-count
